I am a Ph.D. Scholar at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. I am currently working under the supervision of Prof. Debdeep Mukhopadhyay and Dr. Sourangshu Bhattacharya. I obtained an M. Tech. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines) Dhanbad in 2016 and obtained a B. Tech. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Institute of Engineering Management Kolkata under West Bengal University of Technology in 2013. I have also been a Visiting Research Assistant in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University Singapore from August 2017 to January 2018.

I am a member of the Secured Embedded Architecture Laboratory and my primary research interests mainly include Application of Machine Learning in the field of Hardware and Software Security, particularly designing of machine learning based countermeasure for detecting Side-Channel Attacks, Malwares, Ransomwares etc. I also have interest in exploiting side-channel leakages from micro-architectural events to mount side-channel attacks. My other reseach interests include designing of robust machine learning, exploring machine learning attack resistance of Physically Unclonable Functions (PUFs), and anything related to Machine Learning and Security.
